Current NBA Superstars

Nikola Jokic

Nicknamed "The Joker", Nikola Jokic is a Serbian born center that plays for the Denver Nuggets. Often considered one of the greatest offensive players in history, Nikola Jokic is especially known for his amazing passing and court vision, constantly putting up historic numbers. His passing combined with other skills make him of the greats, helping him average a near 30 point triple-double for several seasons. A fun fact about Jokic is that he is widely known to be drafted during a Taco Bell ad. If you try to find a video of him getting drafted, all you will find is an advertisement for the Quesarito.

Nikola Jokic's Accolades:

  • 3x MVP
  • 2023 NBA Champion
  • 8x NBA All-Star
  • 5x All-NBA First Team
  • Lowest Drafted Player to Win an MVP in NBA History (41st overall)

    • Shai Gilgeous-Alexander

    Commonly called SGA, Shai Gilgeous-Alexander is a Canadian born guard that plays for the Oklahoma City Thunder. Shai is known for being the league's best mid-range shooter. This paired along with his smooth handles, crafty finishing, & ability to draw fouls make him a dangerous scorer on all levels of the court. Many fans compare his games to the likes of Jordan & Kobe, and with his efficient, consistent scoring it's easy to see why.

    SGA's Accolades:

    • 2025 NBA Champion
    • 2025 MVP
    • 3x All-NBA First Team
    • 2025 Scoring Title
    • 4x All-Star
